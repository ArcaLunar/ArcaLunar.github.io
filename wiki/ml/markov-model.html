推导

将条件概率表示为联合概率

P(xt+1∣e1:t)=P(xt+1,e1:t)P(e1:t)P(x_{t+1}|e_{1: t})=\frac{P(x_{t+1},e_{1: t})}{P(e_{1: t})} P(xt+1​∣e1:t​)=P(e1:t​)P(xt+1​,e1:t​)​

考虑 xtx_txt​ 这个隐变量，分子就变为对 P(…,xt,… )P(\dots, x_t,\dots)P(…,xt​,…) 进行 sum up

=∑xtP(xt+1,xt,e1:t)P(e1:t)=\frac{\sum_{x_t}P(x_{t+1},x_t,e_{1: t})}{P(e_{1: t})} =P(e1:t​)∑xt​​P(xt+1​,xt​,e1:t​)​
