ekekT=[(I−kkH)ek−−kkVk][(I−kkH)ek−−kkVk]T=[(I−kkH)ek−−kkVk][ek−T(I−kkH)T−VkTkkT]=(I−kkH)ek−ek−T(I−kkH)T−(I−kkH)ek−VkTkkT−kkVkek−T(I−kkH)T+kkVkVkTkkT\begin{aligned} e_ke_k^T&=\Big[(I-k_kH)e_k^--k_kV_k\Big]\Big[(I-k_kH)e_k^--k_kV_k\Big]^T\\ &=\Big[(I-k_kH)e_k^--k_kV_k\Big]\Big[e_k^{-T}(I-k_kH)^T-V_k^Tk_k^T\Big]\\ &=(I-k_kH)e_k^-e_k^{-T}(I-k_kH)^T-(I-k_kH)e_k^-V_k^Tk_k^T\\ &\quad-k_kV_ke_k^{-T}(I-k_kH)^T+k_kV_kV_k^Tk_k^T \end{aligned} ek​ekT​​=[(I−kk​H)ek−​−kk​Vk​][(I−kk​H)ek−​−kk​Vk​]T=[(I−kk​H)ek−​−kk​Vk​][ek−T​(I−kk​H)T−VkT​kkT​]=(I−kk​H)ek−​ek−T​(I−kk​H)T−(I−kk​H)ek−​VkT​kkT​−kk​Vk​ek−T​(I−kk​H)T+kk​Vk​VkT​kkT​​

我们现在来对这个式子求期望，我们先关注中间两项 (I−kkH)ek−VkTkkT(I-k_kH)e_k^-V_k^Tk_k^T(I−kk​H)ek−​VkT​kkT​ 和 kkVkek−T(I−kkH)Tk_kV_ke_k^{-T}(I-k_kH)^Tkk​Vk​ek−T​(I−kk​H)T，我们先求他们的期望

E[(I−kkH)ek−VkTkkT]=(I−kkH)E[ek−VkT]kkT=(I−kkH)E[ek−]E[VkT]kkT=0\begin{aligned} E[(I-k_kH)e_k^-V_k^Tk_k^T]&=\Big(I-k_kH\Big)E\Big[e_k^-V_k^T\Big]k_k^T\\ &=\Big(I-k_kH\Big)E\Big[e_k^-\Big]E\Big[V_k^T\Big]k_k^T=0 \end{aligned} E[(I−kk​H)ek−​VkT​kkT​]​=(I−kk​H)E[ek−​VkT​]kkT​=(I−kk​H)E[ek−​]E[VkT​]kkT​=0​

这是因为噪声 VkV_kVk​ 服从高斯分布，其期望和转置的期望为 000.

同理 E[kkVkek−T(I−kkH)T]=0E[k_kV_ke_k^{-T}(I-k_kH)^T]=0E[kk​Vk​ek−T​(I−kk​H)T]=0，因此可以写成

Pk=E[ekekT]=E[(I−kkH)ek−ek−T(I−kkH)T]+E[kkVkVkTkkT]=(I−kkH)E[ek−ek−T](I−kkH)T+kkE[VkVkT]kkT=(I−kkH)Pk−(I−kkH)T+kkRkkT=(Pk−−kkHPk−)(I−HTkkT)+kkRkkT=Pk−−kkHPk−−Pk−HTkkT+kkHPk−HTkkT+kkRkkT\begin{aligned} P_k=E[e_ke_k^T]&=E\Big[(I-k_kH)e_k^-e_k^{-T}(I-k_kH)^T\Big]+E\Big[k_kV_kV_k^Tk_k^T\Big]\\ &=(I-k_kH)E[e_k^-e_k^{-T}](I-k_kH)^T+k_kE[V_kV_k^T]k_k^T\\ &=(I-k_kH)P_k^-(I-k_kH)^T+k_kRk_k^T\\ &=(P_k^--k_kHP_k^-)(I-H^Tk_k^T)+k_kRk_k^T\\ &=P_k^--k_kHP_k^--P_k^-H^Tk_k^T+k_kHP_k^-H^Tk_k^T+k_kRk_k^T \end{aligned} Pk​=E[ek​ekT​]​=E[(I−kk​H)ek−​ek−T​(I−kk​H)T]+E[kk​Vk​VkT​kkT​]=(I−kk​H)E[ek−​ek−T​](I−kk​H)T+kk​E[Vk​VkT​]kkT​=(I−kk​H)Pk−​(I−kk​H)T+kk​RkkT​=(Pk−​−kk​HPk−​)(I−HTkkT​)+kk​RkkT​=Pk−​−kk​HPk−​−Pk−​HTkkT​+kk​HPk−​HTkkT​+kk​RkkT​​

kkk_kkk​ 是常数矩阵。

由于：

  1. Pk−P_k^-Pk−​ 是协方差矩阵，所以 Pk−=Pk−TP_k^-=P_k^{-T}Pk−​=Pk−T​.
  2. tr(P)=tr(PT)tr(P)=tr(P^T)tr(P)=tr(PT)，而 (kkHPk−)T=Pk−THTkkT=Pk−HTkkT(k_kHP_k^-)^T=P_k^{-T}H^Tk_k^T=P_k^-H^Tk_k^T(kk​HPk−​)T=Pk−T​HTkkT​=Pk−​HTkkT​，因此 tr(kkHPk−)=tr(Pk−HTkkT)tr(k_kHP_k^-)=tr(P_k^-H^Tk_k^T)tr(kk​HPk−​)=tr(Pk−​HTkkT​)

所以有

tr(Pk)=tr(Pk−)−2tr(kkHPk−)+tr(kkHPk−HTkkT)+tr(kkRkkT)\begin{aligned} tr(P_k)&=tr(P_k^-)-2tr(k_kHP_k^-)+tr(k_kHP_k^-H^Tk_k^T)+tr(k_kRk_k^T) \end{aligned} tr(Pk​)​=tr(Pk−​)−2tr(kk​HPk−​)+tr(kk​HPk−​HTkkT​)+tr(kk​RkkT​)​

接下来是对矩阵求导根据法则：

  1. ∇Atr(AB)=BT\nabla_A {tr(AB)}=B^T∇A​tr(AB)=BT
  2. ∇Atr(ABAT)=A(B+BT)\nabla_A tr(ABA^T)=A(B+B^T)∇A​tr(ABAT)=A(B+BT)

我们计算

dtr(Pk)dkk=0−2(HPk−)T+2kkHPk−HT+2kkR=2kk(HPk−HT+R)−2Pk−THT=2(kk(HPk−HT+R)−Pk−HT)=0\begin{aligned} \frac{\mathrm dtr(P_k)}{\mathrm dk_k}&=0-2(HP_k^-)^T+2k_kHP_k^-H^T+2k_kR\\ &=2k_k(HP_k^-H^T+R)-2P_k^{-T}H^T\\ &=2\Bigg(k_k(HP_k^-H^T+R)-P_k^{-}H^T\Bigg)=0 \end{aligned} dkk​dtr(Pk​)​​=0−2(HPk−​)T+2kk​HPk−​HT+2kk​R=2kk​(HPk−​HT+R)−2Pk−T​HT=2(kk​(HPk−​HT+R)−Pk−​HT)=0​

因此

kk=Pk−HTHPk−HT+R\boxed{k_k=\frac{P_k^-H^T}{HP_k^-H^T+R}} kk​=HPk−​HT+RPk−​HT​​

当测量噪声 RRR 很大，kkk_kkk​ 接近 000，我们更相信计算结果而不相信测量结果。

当测量噪声 RRR 很小，kkk_kkk​ 接近 H−1H^{-1}H−1，我们更相信测量结果而不相信计算结果。

误差协方差矩阵 Priori / Posterior Error Covariance Matrix

这一部分是要求 Pk−P_k^-Pk−​，根据定义 Pk−=E[ek−ek−T]P_k^-=E[e_k^-e_k^{-T}]Pk−​=E[ek−​ek−T​]，而 ek−=Xk−X^k−e_k^-=X_k-\hat X_k^-ek−​=Xk​−X^k−​，代入 X^k−=AX^k−1+Buk−1\hat X_k^-=A\hat X_{k-1}+Bu_{k-1}X^k−​=AX^k−1​+Buk−1​ 和 Xk=AXk−1+Buk−1+Wk−1X_k=AX_{k-1}+Bu_{k-1}+W_{k-1}Xk​=AXk−1​+Buk−1​+Wk−1​

ek−=Xk−X^k−=AXk−1+Buk−1+Wk−1−(AX^k−1+Buk−1)=A(Xk−1−X^k−1)+Wk−1=Aek−1+Wk−1\begin{aligned} e_k^-=X_k-\hat X_k^-&=AX_{k-1}+Bu_{k-1}+W_{k-1}-(A\hat X_{k-1}+Bu_{k-1})\\ &=A(X_{k-1}-\hat X_{k-1})+W_{k-1}\\ &=Ae_{k-1}+W_{k-1} \end{aligned} ek−​=Xk​−X^k−​​=AXk−1​+Buk−1​+Wk−1​−(AX^k−1​+Buk−1​)=A(Xk−1​−X^k−1​)+Wk−1​=Aek−1​+Wk−1​​

代入 Pk−P_k^-Pk−​ 的定义式

Pk−=E[ek−ek−T]=E[(Aek−1+Wk−1)(Aek−1+Wk−1)T]=E[(Aek−1+Wk−1)(ek−1TAT+Wk−1T)]⏟Properties of Matrix Transpose=E[Aek−1ek−1TAT+Aek−1Wk−1T+Wk−1ek−1TAT+Wk−1Wk−1T]=AE[ek−1ek−1T]AT+AE[ek−1Wk−1T]+E[Wk−1ek−1T]AT+E[Wk−1Wk−1T]⏟Matrix A and AT are Constant Matrices=APk−1AT+Qk−1\begin{aligned} P_k^-=E[e_k^-e_k^{-T}]&=E\Big[(Ae_{k-1}+W_{k-1})(Ae_{k-1}+W_{k-1})^T\Big]\\ &=\underbrace{E\Big[(Ae_{k-1}+W_{k-1})(e_{k-1}^TA^T+W_{k-1}^T)\Big]}_ {\scriptsize\text{Properties of Matrix Transpose}}\\ &=E\Big[Ae_{k-1}e_{k-1}^TA^T+Ae_{k-1}W_{k-1}^T+W_{k-1}e_{k-1}^TA^T+W_{k-1}W_{k-1}^T\Big]\\ &=AE[e_{k-1}e_{k-1}^T]A^T +AE[e_{k-1}W_{k-1}^T]\\&\quad\underbrace{+E[W_{k-1}e_{k-1}^T]A^T+E[W_{k-1}W_{k-1}^T]}_ {\scriptsize\text{Matrix \(A\) and \(A^T\) are Constant Matrices}}\\ &=AP_{k-1}A^T+Q_{k-1} \end{aligned} Pk−​=E[ek−​ek−T​]​=E[(Aek−1​+Wk−1​)(Aek−1​+Wk−1​)T]=Properties of Matrix TransposeE[(Aek−1​+Wk−1​)(ek−1T​AT+Wk−1T​)]​​=E[Aek−1​ek−1T​AT+Aek−1​Wk−1T​+Wk−1​ek−1T​AT+Wk−1​Wk−1T​]=AE[ek−1​ek−1T​]AT+AE[ek−1​Wk−1T​]Matrix A and AT are Constant Matrices+E[Wk−1​ek−1T​]AT+E[Wk−1​Wk−1T​]​​=APk−1​AT+Qk−1​​

所以先验误差协方差矩阵为

Pk−=APk−1AT+Qk−1\boxed{P_k^-=AP_{k-1}A^T+Q_{k-1}} Pk−​=APk−1​AT+Qk−1​​

有了这个，我们就可以计算出后验估计了。不过在公式里，我们用到了后验误差协方差矩阵，我们该怎么更新呢？

联系上一部分推导的式子，我们代入 kkk_kkk​ 进行化简

Pk=Pk−−kkHPk−−Pk−HTkkT+kkHPk−HTkkT+kkRkkT=Pk−−kkHPk−−Pk−HTkkT+kk(HPk−HT+R)kkT=Pk−−kkHPk−−Pk−HTkkT+Pk−HTkkT=Pk−−kkHPk−=(I−kkH)Pk−\begin{aligned} P_k&=P_k^--k_kHP_k^--P_k^-H^Tk_k^T+k_kHP_k^-H^Tk_k^T+k_kRk_k^T\\ &=P_k^--k_kHP_k^--P_k^-H^Tk_k^T+k_k(HP_k^-H^T+R)k_k^T\\ &=P_k^--k_kHP_k^--P_k^-H^Tk_k^T+P_k^-H^Tk_k^T\\ &=P_k^--k_kHP_k^-\\ &=(I-k_kH)P_k^- \end{aligned} Pk​​=Pk−​−kk​HPk−​−Pk−​HTkkT​+kk​HPk−​HTkkT​+kk​RkkT​=Pk−​−kk​HPk−​−Pk−​HTkkT​+kk​(HPk−​HT+R)kkT​=Pk−​−kk​HPk−​−Pk−​HTkkT​+Pk−​HTkkT​=Pk−​−kk​HPk−​=(I−kk​H)Pk−​​

所以有

Pk=(I−kkH)Pk−\boxed{P_k=(I-k_kH)P_k^-} Pk​=(I−kk​H)Pk−​​

总结

一般来说，使用卡尔曼滤波器有如下步骤，这五个方程也是卡尔曼滤波的五大方程。

Step Formula
（预测）计算先验估计 X^k−=AX^k−1\hat X_k^-=A\hat X_{k-1}X^k−​=AX^k−1​
（预测）计算先验误差协方差矩阵 Pk−=APk−1AT+QP_k^-=AP_{k-1}A^T+QPk−​=APk−1​AT+Q
（校正）计算卡尔曼增益 kk=Pk−HTHPk−HT+Rk_k=\frac{P_k^-H^T}{HP_k^-H^T+R}kk​=HPk−​HT+RPk−​HT​
