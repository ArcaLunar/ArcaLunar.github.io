Remark 2.

The meaning of the C.I.: The C.I. obtained with confidence level β\betaβ has a probability of β\betaβ to cover the unknown mean μ\muμ. This is the range of values you expect your estimate to fall between if you redo your test, within a certain level of confidence.

Remark 3.
