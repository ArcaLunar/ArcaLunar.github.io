概率论入门

Stirling’s Formula

$\text{This formula is to provide an approximation of }n!.\\text{For large value of }n,\text{ it can be shown that}$

$$
\boxed{n!\approx \sqrt{2n\pi}\big(\frac{n}{e}\big)^n=\sqrt{2\pi e}\big(\frac{n}{e}\big)^{n+\frac{1}{2}}}
$$

$\text{From this formula, we have}$

$$
C_{2n}^{n}=\binom{2n}{n}=\frac{(2n)!}{n!n!}\approx \frac{\sqrt{4n\pi}\big(\frac{2n}{e}\big)^{2n}}{\sqrt{2n\pi}(\frac{n}{e})^n\cdot\sqrt{2n\pi}(\frac{n}{e})^n}
$$

$\text{Thus we have}$

$$
C_{2n}^{n}\approx \frac{2^{2n}}{\sqrt{\pi n}}
$$
