$\textbf{Sum of Subspaces}$

$\textbf{定义}$

$\textbf{Definition.}$ 令 $U_1, U_2,\cdots, U_m$ 是 $V$ 的 $\text{subspaces}$，那么

$$
U_1+U_2+\cdots+U_m={u_1+u_2+\cdots+u_m:u_1\in U_1, u_2\in U_2,\cdots,u_m\in U_m}
$$

$\textbf{Theorem.}\text{ Sum of subspaces is the smallest containing subspace.}$

Suppose $U_1,U_2,\ldots,U_m$ are subspaces of $V$. Then $U_1+\ldots+U_m$ is the smallest subspace of $V$ containing $U_1,U_2,\ldots,U_m$.

$\textbf{Direct Sum}$

$\textbf{Definition. }$ $U_1+U_2+\cdots+U_m$ 是 Direct Sum，当且仅当其中的任意一个元素可以且只能被表示为唯一一种 $u_1+u_2+\cdots+u_m$，记作 $U_1\oplus U_2\oplus\cdots\oplus U_m$.

$\textbf{Theorem. }\text{Direct Sum } 判定法则：0=u_1+u_2+\ldots+u_m\ 的表示法是存在且唯一的.$

$\textbf{Proof. }$根据定义，如果 $U_1+U_2+\cdots+U_m$ 是 $\text{Direct Sum}$，那么 $0$ 的表示法就是唯一的。

接下来证明：当 $0$ 的表示法唯一时，$\sum U_i$ 是 $\text{Direct Sum}$。反证法，如果不是 $\text{Direct Sum}$，我们先令

$$
v=u_1+u_2+\ldots+u_m\in \sum U_i,\ u_i\in U_i
$$

那么由假设，$v$ 有两种表示方法，设第二种是：

$$
v=v_1+v_2+\ldots+v_m,\ v_i\in U_i,\ v_i\ne u_i
$$

相减，得

$$
0=(u_1-v_1)+(u_2-v_2)+\ldots+(u_m-v_m)
$$

所以，可以推出 $u_i-v_i=0$，即 $u_i=v_i$，矛盾！

$\textbf{Finite-Dimensional Vector Spaces}$

$\textbf{Span and Linear Independence}$

$\textbf{Definition. } \textit{Linear combination}\text{ of a list of vectors } v_i \text{ is a vector } u \text{ of form}$

$$
u=a_1v_1+a_2v_2+a_3v_3+\ldots+a_mv_m, \text{where }a_i\in F
$$

$\textbf{Definition. }$ $span(v_1,v_2,\ldots,v_m)={a_1v_1+a_2v_2+a_3v_3+\ldots+a_mv_m:a_i\in F,v_i\in V}$

$\textit{The span of the empty list is defined to be } {0}$.

$\textbf{Theorem. }\text{Span is the smallest subspace containing all the vectors in the list.}$

$\textbf{Proof. }\text{First, we show }span(v_i) \text{ is a subspace of }V. \text{ The}\textit{ additive identity}\text{ is in }span(v_i), \text{ since}$

$$
0=0v_1+0v_2+\ldots+0v_m
$$

$\text{Also, }span(v_i) \text{ is closed } \textit{under addition}\text{, because}$

$$
(c_1v_1+c_2v_2+\ldots+c_mv_m)+(a_1v_1+a_2v_2+\ldots+a_mv_m)=(c_1+a_1)v_1+(c_2+a_2)v_2+\ldots+(c_m+a_m)v_m
$$

$\text{Furthermore, }span(v_i)\text{ is closed under }\textit{scalar multiplication}\text{, because}$

$$
\lambda(a_1v_1+a_2v_2+\ldots+a_mv_m)=\lambda a_1v_1+\lambda a_2v_2+\ldots+\lambda a_mv_m
$$

$\text{Thus, }span(v_i)\text{ is a subspace of }V.$
